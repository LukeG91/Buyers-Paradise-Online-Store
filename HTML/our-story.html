Our Story

We are a group of friends who have a passion for fashion and designer clothing items. We have been friends for over 20 years and we decided in January on 2018 to open an online store selling brands and clothing items that we love.


We were apprehensive in the beggining as there are many online stores out there and some major competition. Our passion and love for designer clothing helped us to overcome these fears and as a group we deicded to pursue our idea of selling clothing online.


We have been going for just over 2 years now and we have grown tremendously in that time. Our customers love our products and we love being able to provide our customers with top quality, high fashion desinger clothes and shoes.

How we started

We were sitting around a table at University one afternoon having lunch and we were looking at clothing items for sale online on our cell phones and one of our friends at the table, Nick said "wouldn't it be awesome to be able to have our own store and be able to supply the shoes and clothing items that we love to our customers."


That sentence spoken by Nick turned into a reality 2 months later. After many nights of discussions and brainstorming we decided to reach out to potenital suppliers and soon realised that we were able to provide the shoes and clothing items that we wanted to.


I(Luke) have a passion for coding and decided to teach myself how to code in order to be able to build our online store. I spent a month studying day and night and I was able to have our site up and running in just over 4 weeks. I am very proud to announce that our online store now gets updated regularly with new stock items as the demand for the products we sell is very high and we are so grateful to our customers for being so loyal to our brand.
